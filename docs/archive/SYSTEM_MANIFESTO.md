# The ChefIApp Manifesto (v1.0)

> "You don't just have a TPV. You have an operating system for hospitality, with Truth as the foundation."

## 🌐 The 5 Living Layers

The system is not a collection of pages, but an ecosystem of distinct realities:

1.  **TPV (The Heart - GOLD)**: 
    *   **Status**: 100% Real.
    *   **Role**: Operational Motor.
    *   **Truth**: Offline-first, Reconciled, Error-proof. *Nothing fake allowed here.*

2.  **Setup / Onboarding (The Birth - REAL)**:
    *   **Status**: 100% Real (Backend Connected).
    *   **Role**: Identity & Configuration.
    *   **Truth**: Persists valid business data. Blocks lies via Health Checks.

3.  **Public Page (The Face - REAL/LIMITED)**:
    *   **Status**: Real Content, Preview Interaction.
    *   **Role**: Customer Verification.
    *   **Truth**: Renders real menu/prices. Honestly limits checkout (marked "Preview") rather than faking transactions.

4.  **AppStaff (The Nervous System - PREVIEW)**:
    *   **Status**: Honest Prototype (High Fidelity).
    *   **Role**: Team Psychology (Worker/Manager/Owner).
    *   **Truth**: Visuals are production-grade. Logic is mocked but explicitly labeled. It models *behavior*, not *data* (yet).

5.  **Dashboard (The Brain - WIP)**:
    *   **Status**: Static Placeholder.
    *   **Role**: Aggregation.
    *   **Truth**: Awaits real signal from TPV/Staff.

## 🔗 The Grand Unification (v1.1)

The wiring is pre-destined:
*   **Public -> TPV**: Customer orders flow into the Operational Heart.
*   **TPV -> AppStaff**: Operational events trigger Human Tasks.
*   **AppStaff -> Owner**: Human performance feeds Governance metrics.

## ❤️ The Core Philosophy

*   **Verdade como fundação** (Truth as foundation).
*   **UI como consequência** (UI is a consequence).
*   **Offline como regra** (Offline as a rule).
*   **Governança separada** (Separated Governance).
*   **Psicologia respetada** (Respected Psychology).
