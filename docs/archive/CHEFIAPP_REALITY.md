# ChefIApp Reality: The Operational Constitution
**Status:** ACTIVE
**Goal:** Operational Clarity (Sofia First)

## The Single Truth
ChefIApp is NO LONGER a generic SaaS platform project.
ChefIApp IS now the operational system for **Sofia Gastrobar**.

Anything that does not serve Sofia is **DORMANT** or **FORBIDDEN**.

---

## 🟢 ACTIVE (The Living Reality)
These systems are **alive** and must work flawlessly for Sofia.
*   **Menu Management:** Creating and editing items with photos/prices.
*   **Order Taking (TPV):** Waiter operational flow.
*   **Kitchen Display (KDS):** Seeing orders in the kitchen.
*   **Checkout:** Closing tables and registering payments.
*   **Staff Identity:** Waiter/Manager logins.

## 🟡 DORMANT (The Sleeping Giants)
These systems exist but are **put to sleep** to reduce cognitive load. Do not touch, do not fix, do not think about them.
*   **Billing SaaS Engine:** Stripe subscriptions, plans, invoices. (We manually onboard Sofia).
*   **Multi-tenant Isolation:** We assume single-tenant operation for now.
*   **GoldMonkey AI Intelligence:** No automated insights or suggestions.
*   **Marketing/Landing Pages:** The "sell" is irrelevant, only the "use".

## 🔴 FORBIDDEN (The Anti-Patterns)
Strictly prohibited actions during Phase K.
*   **Structural Refactoring:** No moving files, no "clean architecture" passes.
*   **New Feature Dev:** No "cool ideas". Only pain killers for Sofia.
*   **Microservices Extraction:** The Monolith stays until Phase L.
*   **Automated Testing of Dormant Systems:** Tests for dormant features can fail.

---

## 🧭 The Operational Focus
Our sole metric of success is:
**"Did Sofia successfully serve a table tonight?"**

If the answer is YES, the architecture is correct.
If the answer is NO, we fix only what stopped her.

> "We are not building a system. We are running a restaurant."
