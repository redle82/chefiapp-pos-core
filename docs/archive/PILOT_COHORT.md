## Onboarding Protocol (The "White Glove")

1.  **Deploy:** Set up dedicated environment (or shared instance).
2.  **Hardware:** Verify they have a tablet/laptop.
3.  **Menu:** We transcribe their menu PDF manually (Don't make them do it).
4.  **Training:** 1-hour physical or Zoom walkthrough.
5.  **Go-Live:** We sit with them for the first service (remote or physical).
